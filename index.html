All about Hostout

What we do

Hostout connects people together (host and hostee) for hangout and events. You can get to attend the event of your choice just by searching or checking events that matches your profile tags.

How to host an event

Click on host an event to get started

Add an event title image or thumbnail

Add a restaurant or event location

Add event fees and food & drinks menu

Create #tags for your event or send out invites

Click on create and share your event link


How to attend an event

Explore and search for events you like

Search with a #tag or related words

Choose from our list of events to join by IV or link

Check the event description menu for payments

Add menus you would like to take from the menu list

Click on register now to proceed to payments

Complete your payment and wait for the event
